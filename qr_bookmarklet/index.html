QR Bookmarklet

How to use:

  • So...you're hangin' out with your friends.
  • You've got your phone and you want them to see this cool article / page / post / whatever.
  • Instead of a public recitation of digital poetry comprising of "ache tee tee pee...blah blah dot com"... "pound.. then go down to the bottom"... ugh.
  • Hit this bookmarklet and generate a QR code that takes your friend straight to the page that you're on.

How to install:

  • Click on this link (the page won't change in your browser)
  • Add a bookmark for this page.
  • Edit the bookmark and delete everything up to and including the "#" symbol.
  • You now have a bookmarklet! Yay! You can now click the bookmarklet to share any page that you're on.
