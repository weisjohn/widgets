jQuery.tweeze.js

A jQuery plugin to help retrieve content from the Twitter Search API.

Features

  • Extremely flexible configuration.
  • Pretty dates (thanks John Resig!)
  • Linkified tweets (thanks Jeremy Parrish (rrish.org)
